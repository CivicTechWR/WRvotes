# Registering on the Voters' List

You can Elections Ontario's tool, [Register To Vote](https://vreg.registertovoteon.ca/en/home), to check whether you are on the voters' list. If your information is missing or incorrect, you are able to update your information directly on the [Register To Vote](https://vreg.registertovoteon.ca/en/home) website. Alternatively, the [Register To Vote](https://vreg.registertovoteon.ca/en/home) website offers [a paper application form](https://www.registertovoteon.ca/voters-list/voter-registration-forms).

Note that in order to amend your information on the voters' list you
will have to provide appropriate [voter
identification](https://www.ontario.ca/laws/regulation/130304). 

The [Register To Vote](https://vreg.registertovoteon.ca/en/home) website also allows you to add yourself to the [Register of Absentee Voters](https://vreg.registertovoteon.ca/en/search/disclaimer?mode=absentee), or if you are 16 or 17, the [Register of Future Voters](https://vreg.registertovoteon.ca/en/search/disclaimer?mode=searchFuture).
