# Election Issues

These pages provide background on some of the major policy areas that
come up in municipal elections in Waterloo Region. Understanding the
issues can help you decide which candidates best represent your
priorities.

- [Housing](./housing): Social housing, zoning, affordability, and
  the Region's role in housing policy.
- [Transportation](./transportation): Grand River Transit, the ION
  light rail, roads, and active transportation.
- [Infrastructure](./infrastructure): Water and wastewater systems,
  roads, buildings, and how municipalities plan and fund capital assets.
- [Budgets and Taxation](./budgets-and-taxation): How property taxes
  work, what councils decide, and how municipalities fund services.
